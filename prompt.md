# Prompt Iterations

## Initial Version
You are a customer support assistant.
Write a polite, professional, and concise email reply to the customer message below.

## Revision 1
You are a customer support assistant.
Write a polite, professional, and concise email reply to the customer message below.
Clearly acknowledge the customer's issue and explain the next step.

## Revision 2
You are a customer support assistant.
Write a polite, professional, and concise email reply to the customer message below.
Clearly acknowledge the customer's issue, explain the next step, and avoid making promises you cannot verify.
If the message involves legal, medical, or safety concerns, recommend escalation to a human support specialist.

## What Changed
- In Revision 1, I added an instruction to clearly acknowledge the customer's issue and explain the next step.
- In Revision 2, I added instructions to avoid unverifiable promises and to escalate high-risk cases to a human support specialist.

## Why I Changed It
- The initial version could produce replies that sounded too generic.
- The later versions were designed to make responses more helpful, more specific, and safer for sensitive cases.

## Expected Improvements
- The revised prompts should produce clearer and more actionable replies.
- The final version should handle vague or high-risk customer messages more carefully.
